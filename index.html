07 — Compliance

Certified
and compliant.

ADR
Dangerous goods — classes 3, 6, 8 and 9.
We carry dangerous goods under the ADR agreement, covering classes 3 (flammable liquids), 6 (toxic), 8 (corrosive) and 9 (miscellaneous). Drivers hold valid ADR certificates and equipment is inspected on schedule.
CMR
Insured carriage under the CMR Convention.
Every international shipment travels under the CMR Convention, with CMR insurance covering the goods in transit. You receive a CMR consignment note as proof of carriage and of the cargo's condition.
Euro 6
Low-emission fleet as standard.
Our tractor units meet the Euro 6 emissions standard — the current EU benchmark for low NOx and particulate output — keeping the fleet clean and welcome in low-emission zones across Europe.
ECD
Cleaning document issued per dispatch.
An ECD (European Cleaning Document) certifies that the tank was professionally cleaned before loading. It's issued per dispatch and travels with the shipment as proof of hygiene.
